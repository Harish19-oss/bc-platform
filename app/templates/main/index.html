About This Platform

The Breast Cancer Integrated Bioinformatics Platform brings together curated gene, protein, mutation, pathway, and drug-repurposing data from NCBI, Ensembl, ClinVar, TCGA, Reactome, KEGG, GEO, and PubMed into a single searchable interface. It was built to make cross-referencing breast cancer research data faster and more intuitive for researchers, students, and clinicians.
